Raster Image Art

This raster image was created in Adobe Photoshop and it has an image of a place that I visited with my family. I took a picture because of the way the building made it look like a garden even though it was in the big city. I decided that I wanted to select some parts of the background with different colors to show how the image looks under different effect or lights.

Vector Image Art

This is a vector image that I made using the rectangle tool and eclipse tool to create a cat. I wanted to make either a dog or cat, so I started with a cat to represent my avatar.

Christmas Audio

The moment that I wanted to represent with this sound was a busy christmas street with last minute shopping. In the beginning I wanted a person to be walking out of the subway station, into the busy streets of the city. Where christmas music was all around and people were buying presents last minute.
